Featured Site: Santa’s Secret

A bespoke Christmas decorations store in Bungendore, NSW. They wanted a website that matched their personality — warm, magical, and nothing like a generic template.

What they needed

Santa’s Secret creates unique, handcrafted Christmas decorations and village scenes. Their shop is full of personality — and they wanted a website that reflected that magic. No cookie-cutter templates, no off-the-shelf themes. Just something warm, playful and truly theirs.

What we delivered

We designed a custom site from scratch, complete with gentle interactive falling snow, a fresh logo treatment, opening hours, social links and a beautifully presented photo gallery showcasing their decorations and village displays.

We set up their Google Maps listing, helped them verify their Google Business profile, added an online contact form and made sure everything loads fast on mobile.

Ongoing support

Their site is hosted and monitored through 3LS, always up and always secure. Built in pure HTML, it’s fast, stable and effectively hack-proof. Any updates — new photos, tweaks, hours changes — are handled quickly as part of their ongoing plan.
