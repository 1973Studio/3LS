Our work: Real businesses, real results

Kangaroo Valley Retreat

A boutique bed and breakfast with no website. We built them a full custom site that showcased their stunning property, with special emphasis on their signature 7-foot bathtub. Professional photo galleries, accommodation details, and booking forms.

Result: Their first proper web presence. Bookings came through online. Built in the early days of web design, and it still worked.

Stepup2Fitness

A personal trainer running sessions from home wanted to scale. We built him a professional website that positioned him as a serious gym owner — class schedules, trainer profiles, membership info, and sign-up systems.

Result: He got his gym space. Built a community. The website gave him the credibility and infrastructure to scale from solo sessions to a proper business with multiple staff.

Santa's Secret

A bespoke Christmas decorations store wanted a website that matched their personality — warm, magical, nothing like a generic template. We designed a custom site with interactive falling snow, fresh logo treatment, opening hours, and a beautifully presented photo gallery.

We set up their Google Maps listing, helped verify their Google Business profile, added a contact form, and made sure everything loads fast on mobile.

Result: A website that feels genuinely theirs. Hosted and monitored through 3LS, always up and always secure.
